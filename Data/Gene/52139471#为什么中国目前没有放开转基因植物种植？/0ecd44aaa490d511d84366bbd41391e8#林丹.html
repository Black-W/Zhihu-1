题主的前提“欧美已经发展了数年转基因技术和种植” 是错误的 ，欧美国家每个都有自己独特的关于转基因种植的政策。下面仅仅以G8国家中的 美，英，法，德，意大利，日本，加拿大 为例进行说明。资料来源为 Restrictions on Genetically Modified Organisms ，如果有最新的政策更新，欢迎指正。同样的，中国的转基因政策也需要针对自己的国情，而不是照抄某国的政策。

1. 美国：转基因植物大面积种植，无专门针对转基因植物的法规
资料来源：
Restrictions on Genetically Modified Organisms: United States
【The United States does not have any federal legislation that is specific to genetically modified organisms (GMOs)
...
Compared to other countries, regulation of GMOs in the US is relatively favorable to their development. GMOs are an economically important component of the biotechnology industry, which now plays a significant role in the US economy . 】

2. 英国：虽然没有法律条文禁止转基因植物种植，但事实上没有商业化的转基因作物的种植，进口的转基因作用主要作为饲料和食品加工。
资料来源：
Restrictions on Genetically Modified Organisms: England and Wales
【The UK is generally viewed as having a restrictive approach towards genetically modified organisms (GMOs) and crops; however, recently there appears to have been a slow shift toward greater acceptance of them. [1] Genetically modified (GM) crops are currently not grown commercially in the UK, but they are imported. These crops are primarily used in animal feed and a few food products. [2]

Each application for GM products is determined on a case-by-case basis , and includes consideration of all scientific information available, with the protection of public health and the environment being overriding priorities.
】

3. 法国：对转基因作物种植和使用有极为严格的限制，无商业化的转基因作物种植。
资料来源：
Restrictions on Genetically Modified Organisms: France
【France is considered to be very restrictive on genetically modified organisms (GMOs). [1] Though some aspects of French legislation ostensibly promote a balanced approach to GMOs by guaranteeing the “freedom to consume and produce with or without genetically modified organisms,” [2] French authorities have generally not been favorable to agricultural biotechnology. [3] Although France remains very active in GMO laboratory research, [4] and imports large amounts of GM crops to feed its livestock, [5] there is a complete absence of commercially grown GMOs in French agriculture, [6] and the last French open-field GMO research project ended in July 2013. [7] 】

4. 德国：基于欧盟框架下的转基因管理条例，无商业化转基因作物种植
资料来源：
Restrictions on Genetically Modified Organisms: Germany
【
German legislation on genetically modified organisms (GMOs) operates within the framework of European Union (EU) law.... As a result of these stringencies and of the banning of MON810 maize in 2009, [4] no genetically modified (GM) plants have been cultivated in Germany in recent years as agricultural crops . [5] Even the number of plantings for research purposes has dwindled from about five hundred in the year 2000 to seventy-five in 2012. [6] In 2013 no experimental planting was carried out owing to persistent sabotage by anti-GMO activists. [7]
】

5. 意大利：对转基因作物种植有严格控制，每个省有自己单独的转基因政策，但总体上转基因作物没有大规模种植

Canada is the third largest producer of genetically modified organisms (GMOs) in the world. [1] It is one of the largest producers of GM canola oil and other GM crops include maize, soybean, and beet.

中国的转基因作物种植情况
资料来源：
Restrictions on Genetically Modified Organisms: China

据农业部2013年4月27日公布的数据显示，中国已发出七个转基因作物的生物安全证书，，包括番茄（1997），棉花（1997），矮牵牛（1999） ，甜椒和辣椒（1999），木瓜（2006年），米（2009年）和玉米（2009年）。其中，棉花已大面积种植。根据农业部统计，截至2010年，中国有三百三十万公顷的转基因棉花种植面积，以及一些转基因木瓜的种植。而其他转基因作物并没有得到广泛种植。


根据2012年统计数据, 中国有发展中国家中最大的，世界第六大的，转基因作物的种植面积.


棉花，大豆，玉米，油菜获得了中国的转基因进口许可证。其中，仅棉花被允许种植，其他作物只用作为原料。根据农业部2011年统计数据，进口转基因大豆已占国内大豆消费的2/3.
