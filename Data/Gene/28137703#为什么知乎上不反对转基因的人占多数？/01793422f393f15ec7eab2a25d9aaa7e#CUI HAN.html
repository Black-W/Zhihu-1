———————————————-Collectively, the studies cited in this review clearly indicate that GM crops are prospering and have the potential to spread across the globe. The studies mention no direct harm to either human or animal health as a consequence of the consumption of GM food or feed. However, there remain concerns regarding the long-term usage of GM food and feed.
这篇文章回顾了近年来几十篇关于研究基因改造食物的危害的文章，结论是：按照目前的检测手段，没有发现任何基因改造食物有危害（当然，为了严谨，文章还说，长期食用基因改造食物的危害目前还未知）。
